About Us

Our Chefs

Chefs at WeeBowl are weebs themselves. Studying every recipe from Shokugeki no Soma, saying 'Naruhodo' after learning every recipe, our chefs have crossed the limits of japnese cuisine to serve the best food to our weeb customers. They know more than anyone that weebs deserve the best.

Our Craft

The art of making Japnese cuisine is engraved in our tools. Using the right tools for right purpose only ensures that our craft does not lack perfection. We use Deba-bocho for fish, Nakiri-bocho for vegetables and Yanagi-ba for Sashimi.

Our Bowl

We serve only the best in our bowl. Fresh Salmon, fresh seaweed, fresh herbs from Japan are used to make our recipes. We believe that only the best food should be served in bowl, rest is all cat food.
